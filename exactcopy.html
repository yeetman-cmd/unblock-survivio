How to Play

The goal of surviv.io is to be the last player standing. You only live once per game - there is no respawn!

2D PUBG

If you've played other battle royale games like PUBG, Fortnite or H1Z1, then you're already halfway there! Think of surviv.io as 2D PUBG (with slightly less desync and more chicken).

Loot and Kill

You'll begin the game with no items other than a simple backpack. Move around the map to find loot: weapons, ammo, scopes, and medical items. Eliminate other players and you can take their loot!

Red = Bad!

Players aren't the only thing that can hurt you. The deadly red zone will move in from the sides of the map and deal increasingly greater damage if you stand in it. Keep an eye on the map and stay safe.

What's New!

August 13, 2018

Low-key update

Key rebinding has arrived. Bind shoot to Spacebar, reload to Page Up, or for you crazies out there, Interact to E. Certain keys are disabled due to browser conflicts (see the changelog for the full list).

As a bonus option, you can even share your keybinds with other players! Click the "Share" button in the main menu keybind customization screen and you'll get a keybind code, as well as a box for loading codes.

Our next big content push is slated for later this month or early next month. Keep up on the latest through our Discord server.

Aug, 11, 2018

Burnout

Air drop supplies have returned to normal levels, and that means it's the end of the flare gun event.

Thank you to everyone who participated, and a special shout-out to the event's top fragging squad: Not d0bu, Aimbot, 1337 ChefDo, and Chisp official, who achieved a staggering 48-kill game!

Aug. 9, 2018

Meteor shower

An unexpected surplus at the PARMA supply depot means EVERYTHING MUST GO! For a limited time only, squad mode will feature massively increased flare gun spawns. These increased spawn rates will last until supplies run out, so get in now before the AWMs and Garands are all gone!

July 26, 2018

Air drop it like it's hot

The good folks at PARMA are now making regular supply runs over the Island, releasing air drops with precious cargo. These special crates contain rare quality loot, including extra rare weapons and items that can only be found in the air drop. The cargo plane will make regularly timed visits to the Island, but you can also get air drops on demand by finding and firing a flare gun.

And presenting the long awaited arrival of melee weapons! Scattered about the Island you'll discover various hand-to-hand tools, such as the wood axe, which deals massive damage to obstacles and can clear those pesky green ammo crates. You may also stumble upon a cache of knives, which offer a small damage upgrade over fists, and a large upgrade in style points.

We're also proud to announce our Discord server hit 40,000 members! Come join our Discord community, where you can share your chicken dinner stories, find a new squadmate, or contribute to our ever-growing collection of user-created concepts and art.
